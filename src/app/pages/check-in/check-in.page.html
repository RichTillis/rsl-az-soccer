Check In

Each team player will need a laminated player card and coaches will need a pass as well. All players must have a medical clearance form(notarized for out of state teams). Travel approval forms are necessary for any team from outside of region 4 and international teams.

  • Player Pass
  • Medical Clearance
  • Travel Paperwork if applicable (Outside Region 4)

Local Team Check-In

In Town teams will check in Tuesday, January 10th & Wednesday, January 15th at Kino Sports Complex at 2500 E. Ajo Way from 4pm – 9pm.

Traveling Teams Check-In: Friday, January 18th, 2019

Out-of Town teams will check in Friday, January 18th at Kino Sports Complex at 2500 E. Ajo Way from 12pm – 9pm.
