Inclement Weather

Inclement Weather Procedures

In the event of inclement weather, the designated Tournament official will make a decision as to whether the fields should be cleared. If the Tournament official determines that the games need to be stopped due to weather, including lightning (10 mile rule) those present at the site will be instructed to leave the area by the Tournament Official.

Once notified of game play stoppage because of the weather conditions, all present should clear the fields and go to their cars. DO NOT LEAVE THE SITE. A tournament official will inform your coaching staff when games may resume play.

In the case of rain, sleet or light snow, play will continue unless halted by the Tournament official or center referee. Matches may be halted when fields become hazardous as to present a serious potential of injury to players or lasting damage to the fields. The Tournament Official is responsible for determining both the need to stop games and when it is safe to resume match play.

In the event that a match is cancelled due the inclement weather, the score at the time of cancellation will be declared the final score and will be recorded as such if one half of the game has been completed. Matches in progress, but with less than one half completed, will be recorded as a 0-0 tie. The Tournament Official reserves the right to make whatever adjustments may be necessary in game length, relocating the game. etc. to proceed with the schedule following a delay due to inclement weather. This policy is to be followed on a site to site situation. The inclement weather can be at one site but not at another, thus unaffected sites will proceed match play as scheduled.

In the event of inclement weather during the finals on Sunday, the above procedures will be followed. If at all possible, final contest will be decided on the field by completion of the game or by penalty kicks, if time or conditions do not permit game completion. In the case that games are not permitted to resume play to completion, accrued points will be used to determine the outcome of the tournament.

The Tournament Committee, the coaches of the host teams, or sponsors of any of the tournament activities shall not be held responsible for expenses incurred by any team or club if the tournament is cancelled in whole or in part.
